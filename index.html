Biography

I am a Ph.D. candiate in Computer Vision advised by Prof. Lauren Christopher and LingXi Li . My research interests are Image Processing, Signal Processing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Intelligent Transportation Systems and Visual Perception. Moreover, I obtained my master degree of cognitive neuroscience from State Key Laboratory of Cognitive neuroscience and Learning, Beijing Normal University(BNU) in 2019, where I studied motion integration mechanism of primate brain via eye-tracking technology advised by Prof. Dajun Xing. I got my undergradute degree of Electronic Engineering in Chongqing University(CQU) in 2013.
